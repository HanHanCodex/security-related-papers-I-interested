# A Survey of Kernel Fuzzing

**研究方向：** Fuzzing, Kernel

## 📝 原文摘要 (Original Abstract)
> The paper provides a detailed examination of the fundamental concepts, technical architecture, and operational workflows of kernel fuzzing systems. It surveys existing kernel fuzzing methods, highlights the evolution and technological advancements in the field, and proposes a novel taxonomy of current approaches.
